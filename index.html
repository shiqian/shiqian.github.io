在法国如何办理留学回国证明

發表於 2017-12-29 | 分類於 studying abroad

是不是留学结束准备回国工作啦？

反正我在法国呆了三年，还是决定回国啦，去上海工作，办户口必须要这个留学回国证明。

你可以在驻法大使馆教育处的网站非国家公派留法人员办理《留学回国人员证明》须知找到所有必须信息，如所需材料清单，教育处开放时间，如何邮寄材料等等。

这里强调一下关键点：

  • 第一步是在留学生信息登记系统中登记注册。刚登陆这个网站的时候我真的强烈怀疑这个网站的安全性，因为它竟然不是https的。不过办理留学回国人员证明是免费的，所以不涉及网上付款，即使真的因为网络安全问题个人信息泄露那么一丢丢，我也认了。第一次注册时点选右上角的“第一次注册”的radiobox，然后输入护照号码（不需要输入密码），注册成功后系统会给出密码，记录这个密码以便稍后登陆时使用。登陆时点选右上角的“已经注册”radiobox，然后输入护照号码和密码你就可以打开你的登记表页面啦。
  • 可以现场递交材料也可以邮寄材料，但无论哪种方式证明办好后都是以邮寄方式送到你手中。使馆教育处每周二、五下午2:30-5:00接待留学人员办理《留学回国人员证明》。现场递交材料原则上一周内办好寄出，邮寄材料原则上法国境内一个月内办好寄出。我是圣诞节前一周寄出材料，12月29号收到工作人员邮件通知我证明已经寄出请我查收。在这里感谢下老师们的高效工作！
  • 邮寄方式申请办理《证明》，请将申请材料直接寄给片区分管老师，并在信封左上角注明“办理回国证明”字样。地址就写教育处的地址： 29, rue de la Glacière, 75013 Paris。
  • 如有疑问，可以通过邮件或电话联系片区的分管老师。教育处网站上给出了各个片区分管老师的联系方式。不过我发了两封邮件都没有收到回复，建议条件允许还是打电话吧，实时沟通还是快些。
  • 办理《留学回国人员证明》和学历学位认证不是一回事。国外学历学位认证统一由教育部留学服务中心负责办理，留学人员回国后，需要到教育部留学服务中心或其指定的机构申请办理学历学位认证。关于国外学位认证的详细信息，请登录上教育部留学服务中心网站查询。

祝办证顺利！

Browser tabs

Navigate to the tab you want to go

  • Firefox/Chrome: Command + number
    • i.e. Cmd+3 will take you to the third tab across,
    • Cmd+9 to the ninth, etc. (although in Chrome, Cmd+9 will take you to the last tab, no matter what).

Flip to the next or previous tab

  • Firefox, Chrome: Command+Option+Arrow (left or right)
  • Safari: Command+Shift+Arrow (left or right)

How to write a good CV

發表於 2017-12-28 | 分類於 Job hunting

When I studied in France, I used to attend a CV workshop organized by Orange in our engineering shcool. The presenter was a HR specialist and she gave us some very pratical tips for writing CV. I would like to share my notes with you.

1.logo (if possible, put the logo of your school or company where you have been worked at in your CV, so that HR can identify quickly as generally HR only spends 8 seconds to scan your CV)

2.photo

  • in America, photo in CV is forbidden while in Europe it is not mandatory
  • photo should be professional (no beach :) )

3.content

  • a quick summary (two sentences)
  • contact details (include Linkedin)
  • title (your target: eg: Telecom system engineer looking for an internship ….)
  • education
  • technical skills (write it correctly, i.e. JavaScript instead of js; you should really know these skills because most probably you will be asked during the interview. Be honest!)
  • internship (your role, responsibility, achievement, technical skills involved, environment…)
  • school projects
  • languages (first mother tongue, in level (basic, intermediate, fluent, bilingual); to be specific, you can also write, professional language in which company… )
  • volunteering (optional)
  • hobbies (optional)
  • references (if you apply a job in UK, it will be appreciated because they could call your references for recommendation)

4.DIY CV
DON’T use Europass. Instead, use Doyour buzz

Good luck!

How to prepare your interview for a software development engineer position

發表於 2017-12-27 | 分類於 Job hunting

Even though I have decided to transferred from software developer to coordinator, I would like to share some experience for preparing the interview for software development engineer position.

Here are some resources that a HR in Compnay G recommended me after my interview with her.

  • https://oj.leetcode.com/
  • http://codeforces.com/
  • https://www.hackerrank.com/
  • http://www.topcoder.com/
  • http://bigocheatsheet.com/
  • http://www.infoarena.ro/
  • https://www.palantir.com/2011/10/how-to-ace-a-systems-design-interview/
  • https://github.com/checkcheckzz/system-design-interview

That time I was so glad that I received her invitation for a talk. As she is HR, and she mentioned that the interview is just to know each other so I thought maybe I just needed to prepare a nice self-presentaiton and then prepare for the technical interview after the first interview. It turns our that we did presented ourselves to each other but soon she asked what kind of programming language you are good at, I said C and Ruby. Then she asked, are you familiar with data structure? I hesitated for 5 seconds and said yes. I began to worry that she would ask some questions about data structure. Indeed what I was worried really happened! She kept asking me the time complexity and space complexity of different Sorting algorithms… Obvisouly I did not prepare myself for this bomb. Unfortunately and naturally I failed… The nice HR girl tried to comfort me saying that they are quite interested in my background and experience however they also would like the candidate to be excellent in computer science which means you should really grasp data structure, algorithm, system design, etc.

What I want to say here is, prepare yourself and found a solid basis before sending out your CV to a top IT company.

For another thing, according to the reviews on Glassdoor, it is worth to read the book <Cracking the Coding Interview>.

By the way, a friend also recommended me to visit 1point3acres which is quite useful and interesting.

Good luck!

西班牙南部之旅

發表於 2017-12-27 | 分類於 travel

西班牙为何被翻译成西班牙

因为即将回国工作，我决定在离开欧洲前再进行一次说走就走的旅行。鉴于已经略微厌倦大城市千篇一律的广场喷泉和大理石建筑，这次我接受了朋友的建议 —
”去安达鲁西亚吧，那里的风光美不胜收，大冬天的你都可以穿着短袖到处乱逛，当然晚上还是很冷滴…“

OK, 那就去安达鲁西亚吧，那个充满异域风情的西班牙南部自治区。实话说，这是我第一次去西班牙，除了堂吉柯德、斗牛、海鲜饭，以及西班牙语与法语意大利语同属罗曼语系，我对西班牙一无所知。当时一个问号马上冒出，西班牙为何被翻译为西班牙？

英语Spain，法语Espagne，西班牙语España，怎么听也不是西班牙呀？根据一位资深的中国籍沙发客Leo的解释，西班牙国名的中文翻译出自《瀛寰志略》。这本书的作者是清朝的徐继畲（yú），成书于1849年，与魏源的《海国图志》同为中国较早的世界地理志。书中首先以地球为引子介绍了东西半球的概况，之后按亚洲、欧洲、非洲、美洲的顺序依次介绍了世界各国的风土人情。

1843年时任福建布政使、福建通商事务督办的徐继畲来到厦门，次年在与英领事记里布(Gribble Henry)商讨外国人在厦门活动的范围之事时结识了美国传教士雅裨理(Rev. David Abeel)。雅裨理在南洋及中国广州、澳门、厦门生活多年，通晓华文和闽南方言，故受邀担任徐继畲与记里布的传译。出于对地理和历史的兴趣，徐继畲在与雅裨理的数次会晤中探询了世界各地的情况。雅裨理欣然提供了许多与世界历史地理有关的统计资料，并向他讲解西方地图，且帮助他复制用中文标注的外国地图。通过与雅裨理的多次晤谈，徐继畲为编写《瀛寰志略》积累了丰富的材料。

西班牙曾是罗马帝国的一个行省(Hispania，意为伊比利亚半岛)，在古籍中被音译为“日斯巴尼亚”或“以西巴尼亚”。
西班牙双王（卡斯蒂利亚女王伊莎贝拉一世和阿拉贡国王费尔南多二世）统治时期，España开始作为卡斯蒂利亚和阿拉贡联合王国的名字被广泛使用。传说España来源于西班牙某位国王的名字Espan。

西班牙，西班牙语España，发音/espaɲa/。在闽南语、客家话里，“牙”的发音是nga，其中ng可作前缀，发软腭鼻音/ŋ/ 的音，比较接近硬腭鼻音/ɲ/的音。用闽南话来读“西班牙”三个字，和España非常相似。徐继畲参考雅裨理的音译，将之翻译为西班牙。

摘自《瀛寰志略》卷七 - 欧罗巴西班牙国

“西班牙，是班牙、实班牙、斯扁亚、士便、干丝腊，又斯巴尼亚、以西把尼亚、大吕宋。亦欧罗巴大国。东北与佛朗西接壤，东南距地中海…“

注：西班牙曾占据吕宋，因此明朝将之称为大吕宋。

《瀛寰志略》出版后，开启了国人对世界的认知，影响了一代维新人物，如康有为、梁启超等。书中对许多国名、地名、人名的译法也一并流传下来。

How to do self presentation during your interview

發表於 2017-11-21 | 分類於 Job hunting

I have had a lot of interviews during the last 6 years, when looking for an internship, applying to stury aboard, or looking for a job.

Almost in all interviews, I was asked to present myself. Usually the interviewer will do a quick self introduction and then he or they will say “Okay, now it is your turn, tell me about you and why you are here now”.

Here are some suggestions according to my experience.

  1. take the time, calm down, do not speak too quickly
  2. control the sound volume, do not be too loud
  3. show motivation
  4. smiling
  5. put your hands where the interview can see
  6. for every professional experience:
    1. one phrase about enterprise (name and business),
    2. two phases about the project (constraint, team, change),
    3. some details about environment, techniques
    4. my roles, responsibilities
    5. positive point, activement, skills obtained

You can perform a rehearsal with your friend before the interview to check if you remember the outlines and increase your confidence. However, do not rehearsal too much! You do not need to recite each sentence during the interview, instead, you just need to follow the flow of presentation and highlight the key points.

副代词en/y

發表於 2017-11-19 | 分類於 French

en

  1. pronom en = expression de quantité 作直接宾语
    -Tu prends des légumes?
    -Oui, j’en prends.
    -Tu bois du café?
    -Oui, j’en bois.
    -Est-ce-qu’il y a assez de pain?
    -Oui, il y en a beaucoup.

  2. pronom en = de + nom 间接宾语或状语
    Il parle de son bureau.
    -> Il en parle.
    Ils s’occupent de ce project.
    -> Ils s’en occupent.
    Nous avons été ravis de vous revoir.
    -> Nous en avons été ravis.
    如果名词是人，不能用en代替，而应保留de。
    -De qui parlez-vous?Toujours de Marc?
    -Oui, nous parlons toujours de lui.
    Les professeurs sont fiers des étudiants.
    ->Les professeurs sont fiers d’eux.

  3. l’adverb de lieu en
    -Tu sais où est son bureau?
    -Oui, J’en viens.

y

  1. l’adverb de lieu
    On y va tout de suite.
    y = à / dans + lieu

  2. le pronom C.O.I
    -Tu pense a tes études?
    -Oui, j’y pense.
    -Tu t’intéresse à l’informatique?
    -Oui, Je m’y intéresse.
    如果名词是人，不能用en代替，而应保留de。
    -Tu pense à tes parents?
    -Oui, je pense à eux.
