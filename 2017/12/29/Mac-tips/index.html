Browser tabs

Navigate to the tab you want to go

  • Firefox/Chrome: Command + number
    • i.e. Cmd+3 will take you to the third tab across,
    • Cmd+9 to the ninth, etc. (although in Chrome, Cmd+9 will take you to the last tab, no matter what).

Flip to the next or previous tab

  • Firefox, Chrome: Command+Option+Arrow (left or right)
  • Safari: Command+Shift+Arrow (left or right)
