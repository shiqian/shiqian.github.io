How to ace your final interview

發表於 2018-01-01 | 分類於 Job hunting

Just receive the invitation for final interview?

Congratulations! However, do not assume that you have already got the job! You need to keep selling yourself during the final interview and be aware that the same time the interviewer may also try to sell themselves to you.

Here is a very nice article about how to prepare your final interview.
https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/interviewing/how-to-ace-your-final-interview

Here are some tips for but not limited for preparing final interview:

  1. Research the company and position
  2. Bring copies of your resume
  3. Prepare questions to ask your interviewers (i.e. immediate tasks that needs to be addressed, training plan, performance management, KPI, etc)
  4. Keep your body language confident and polite
  5. Dress appropriately
  6. Express your enthusiasm and interest
  7. Follow up with a thank-you email (include the details, you can say “thank you for your clear and patient explanation for the position and the department”, etc. If you do so, normally you will also receive a feedback email with details. It is a very nice interaction.)
